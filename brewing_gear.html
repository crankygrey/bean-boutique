Today's Recipe

Indulge in the art of brewing with a recipe crafted for the finest moments.

Cappuccino Royale with Morning Glory Blend

Ingredients:
- 18g of Morning Glory Blend (freshly ground)
- 180ml of steamed whole milk
- 20ml of vanilla syrup
- A dash of cinnamon for garnish

Instructions:
1. Brew a double shot of espresso using the Morning Glory Blend.
2. Steam the milk to create a rich, velvety texture.
3. Pour the espresso into a cup and add the vanilla syrup.
4. Slowly pour the steamed milk over the espresso.
5. Garnish with a sprinkle of cinnamon for a luxurious finish.
